Overview of Health and Safety Regulations

Scope of the Regulations

Health and Safety regulations are designed to protect the well-being of employees and the public from workplace hazards. These regulations cover risk assessments, safety equipment, and employee training. Employers are required to provide a safe working environment to prevent accidents and illnesses.
