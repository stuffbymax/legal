Overview of Data Privacy Regulations

Scope of the Regulations

Data Privacy regulations, such as GDPR, are designed to protect personal information by requiring companies to handle data responsibly. These rules cover data collection, storage, and sharing. Organizations must ensure transparency, security, and legal compliance to avoid breaches and fines.
